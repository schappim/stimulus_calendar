Swipe navigation

Two-finger horizontal swipe on a trackpad, finger swipe on touch, or pointer drag on a mouse — all advance the calendar one page in either direction. Tap or click an event to interact; double-click for the popover; drag an event chip to move; drag its bottom edge in week/day view to resize. Use ← / → for keyboard navigation.

May 2026

Behind the scenes the pager mounts every view inside a translateX-able track of three pages; the prev and next dates pre-render on first gesture so the swipe shows real content (not a placeholder).
