SMS reminders linked to jobs — auto‑shift on move

Each job carries a child reminder scheduled 24 hours before the start. The reminder is a separate event with extendedProps.kind: 'reminder' and a back‑pointer jobId. When the job moves, an eventDrop handler re‑computes the reminder's time and pushes it through updateEvent — no separate scheduling system needed.

Drag a job (the coloured chip). The dashed purple reminder slides with it. A toast notifies the back office that the SMS schedule was updated.
