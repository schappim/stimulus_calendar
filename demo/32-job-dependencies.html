Job dependencies — rough‑in must precede fit‑off

Each dependent job carries extendedProps.dependsOn: [jobId]. On calendar:eventDrop we look up the dependency and, if the new start lands before the dependency's end, call detail.revert() and warn. The library handles the revert optimistically — the chip snaps back without any extra plumbing.

Try it: drag "Bathroom fit-off" earlier than "Bathroom rough-in" — the drop reverts with a toast.
