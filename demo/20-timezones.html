IANA timezones new

The same fixed UTC instant rendered across multiple named timezones — Australia/Sydney, America/New_York, Europe/London, Asia/Tokyo, UTC. Resolution is DST-aware: Sydney renders at +11:00 in January and +10:00 in July, and the calendar re-anchors when you navigate across a transition.
