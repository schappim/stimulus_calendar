Cross-timezone live sync new

Two calendars share a BroadcastChannel but live in different IANA zones. Drag an event in either window — the same UTC instant arrives in the other window and renders at its own local wall-clock. The wire format is YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM:SS±HH:MM so the receiver knows what offset the wall-clock was relative to and re-anchors accordingly. Change each panel's timezone live from its dropdown.
